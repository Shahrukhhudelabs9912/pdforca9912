## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1: Kya animations PDF mein kaam karenge?**
Nahi. PDF static format hai — animations aur transitions kaam nahi karte. PDF mein slide ka final state dikhta hai. Live presentation ke liye PowerPoint use karo, sharing/printing ke liye PDF.

**Q2: Har slide ek page banega ya multiple slides ek page pe?**
By default har slide ek PDF page banta hai. Agar multiple slides ek page pe chahiye (handout style), PowerPoint mein "Handouts" layout se export karo.

**Q3: Videos aur sound ka kya hoga?**
Videos PDF mein play nahi honge — sirf ek static thumbnail dikhega. Sound bhi nahi aayega. Multimedia content ke liye PowerPoint hi rakhna padega.

**Q4: Kya PDF se wapas editable PPT ban sakti hai?**
Haan, [PDFOrca PDF to PowerPoint](https://pdforca.com/pdf-to-powerpoint) se PDF ko wapas editable PowerPoint mein convert kar sakte ho. Text aur images editable ho jaate hain.

**Q5: Custom fonts convert ke baad change ho jaati hain?**
Agar PowerPoint mein fonts embed ki hain, toh PDF mein sahi rahengi. Embed karne ke liye: `File → Options → Save → Embed fonts in the file`. PDFOrca embedded fonts ko preserve karta hai.
