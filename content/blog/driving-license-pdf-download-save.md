## DigiLocker Kya Hai Aur DL Kyun Wahin Se Le?

DigiLocker Indian government ka official digital document wallet hai. Isme se nikaale gaye documents pe government ka digital signature hota hai, isliye wo har jagah valid hote hain — kisi bhi photo ya normal scan se zyada trusted.

Aap seedha apne phone ki gallery wali photo bhi use kar sakte ho, par usme do dikkat hain:
- Wo blurry ya cropped ho sakti hai, jise portal reject kar de
- Usme koi official verification nahi hoti — ek edited photo aur asli mein farak nahi dikhta

DigiLocker wala PDF in dono problems ko solve karta hai — clean, official, aur verified.

## Step-by-Step: DigiLocker Se Driving License PDF Download Karo

### Step 1: DigiLocker Mein Login Karo

1. DigiLocker app kholo ya digilocker.gov.in pe jao
2. Apne registered mobile number ya Aadhaar se login karo
3. OTP daalo jo aapke phone pe aayega
4. 6-digit security PIN daalo

### Step 2: Driving License Search Karo

1. Home screen pe "Search Documents" ya "Get Documents" pe jao
2. **"Ministry of Road Transport and Highways"** ya seedha **"Driving License"** search karo
3. Issuing state (jahan se DL bana hai) select karo

### Step 3: DL Details Daalo Aur Fetch Karo

1. Apna **Driving License Number** daalo
2. **Date of Birth** daalo (DL pe jaisi likhi hai waisi)
3. Terms accept karke **"Get Document"** pe click karo
4. DigiLocker RTO se aapki details verify karke license fetch karega

### Step 4: PDF Download Karo

1. Fetch hone ke baad DL "Issued Documents" mein aa jaayega
2. Us document pe tap karo
3. Download / PDF icon pe click karke file save karo

Bas — aapke paas ab official, digitally-signed driving license PDF hai.

## DL PDF Ko Form Ke Liye Compress Kaise Kare

Aksar DigiLocker se downloaded PDF ka size zyada hota hai, aur job portals ya government forms pe strict limit hoti hai (200KB – 1MB). Agar aapka DL PDF limit se bada hai:

1. [Compress PDF tool](/compress-pdf) open karo
2. Apna DL PDF upload karo
3. **Recommended** compression se shuru karo — zyadatar 2-5MB limit ke liye kaafi hai
4. Strict limit (200KB – 1MB) ke liye **High compression** use karo
5. Download karke check karo ki license number aur photo saaf dikh rahe hain

Ek 2MB ka DL PDF aksar Recommended pe 300-500KB tak, aur High pe 150-200KB tak aa jaata hai — bina number ya photo dhundhla hue.

## mParivahan App — DigiLocker Ka Alternative

DigiLocker ke alawa ek aur official option hai — **mParivahan app** (Ministry of Road Transport and Highways ki official app). Agar kisi wajah se DigiLocker mein DL fetch nahi ho raha, toh mParivahan try karo:

1. Play Store ya App Store se **mParivahan** app install karo
2. Apne mobile number se register karo aur OTP verify karo
3. "Dashboard" pe **"Virtual DL"** add karo — apna DL number daalo
4. App aapki details verify karke virtual license dikhaayega
5. Isse PDF ya image ke roop mein save/share kar sakte ho

mParivahan ka virtual DL bhi legally valid hai aur traffic police ise accept karti hai. DigiLocker aur mParivahan — dono government ke official platforms hain, isliye kisi third-party website se DL "download" karne ke chakkar mein mat pado; wo assli nahi hota aur aapka data bhi risk mein aata hai.

|-----------|-----------------------------------|---------------|

Ek 2018 ki government advisory mein saaf kaha gaya hai ki traffic police ko in digital copies ko accept karna chahiye. Isliye zyadatar rozana kaamon ke liye digital PDF hi kaafi hai.

## DL Ki Photo Se PDF Kaise Banaye (Agar DigiLocker Available Nahi)

Kabhi-kabhi aapke paas sirf physical driving license hota hai aur DigiLocker ya mParivahan pe record fetch nahi ho raha (naye states ke records kabhi late update hote hain). Aise mein aap khud DL ki achhi PDF bana sakte ho:

1. DL ko achhi roshni mein, flat surface pe rakho
2. Phone se seedha upar se photo kheencho — front aur back dono
3. [JPG to PDF tool](/jpg-to-pdf) kholo
4. Dono photos upload karo, page size **A4 Portrait** rakho
5. Convert karke ek single PDF banao — front page 1, back page 2
6. Zaroorat ho toh [Compress PDF](/compress-pdf) se size limit ke andar laao

**Photo tips:**
- Natural daylight best hai — flash se glare aata hai jo details chhupa deta hai
- Card ko frame mein poora bharo, par edges cut na ho
- Hologram aur signature clear dikhne chahiye
- Angle se nahi, bilkul upar se photo lo taaki card tirchha na dikhe

Yaad rahe — official portals aur verification ke liye DigiLocker/mParivahan wala PDF hi best hai kyunki wo digitally signed hota hai. Photo-wali PDF sirf tab use karo jab official download possible na ho.

## FAQ

Haan. DigiLocker se nikaala gaya DL digitally signed hota hai aur Motor Vehicles Act ke tehat physical license ke barabar legally valid hai. Traffic police aur government portals dono ise accept karte hain.
