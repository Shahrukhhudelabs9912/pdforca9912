## Tips: Best Quality PDF Kaise Banaye Photos Se

1. **Good lighting mein photo lein** — natural light best hai, flash se glare aata hai
2. **Camera ko steady rakhein** — shake se blur aata hai
3. **Document ko flat surface pe rakhein** — wrinkles aur folds se text unclear hota hai
4. **Edges properly cover karein** — poora document frame mein aana chahiye
5. **Dark background use karein** — white paper ke neeche dark surface rakhein taaki edges clearly dikhein
6. **HDR mode off rakhein** — documents ke liye normal mode better hai
7. **Crop extra area** — photo gallery mein pehle extra white space crop karein
