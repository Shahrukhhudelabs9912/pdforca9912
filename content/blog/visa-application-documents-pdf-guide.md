Visa apply karna ek detailed process hai — aur documents ki list dekh ke hi ghabrahat hoti hai. Passport copy, bank statements, invitation letter, hotel booking, flight itinerary, employment letter — sab alag-alag files hain. Embassy ya consulate ko organized, clear documents chahiye. Agar documents sahi format mein nahi hue, visa reject ho sakta hai ya delay ho sakta hai.

Is guide mein bataunga ki visa application ke liye documents kaise prepare karo, PDF mein kaise organize karo, aur embassy ki requirements kaise meet karo.

## Visa Application Mein Kaunse Documents Chahiye

Har country ki requirements alag hoti hain, lekin generally ye documents lagte hain:

### Identity Documents
- Passport (first aur last page, validity page)
- Old passports (agar hain)
- Aadhaar card / Voter ID (address proof)
- Passport size photos (specifications vary by country)

### Financial Documents
- Bank statements (last 3-6 months)
- ITR (Income Tax Returns) — last 2-3 years
- Salary slips (last 3 months)
- Fixed deposit certificates (agar hain)
- Property documents (agar applicable)

### Travel Documents
- Flight itinerary (confirmed ya tentative)
- Hotel bookings
- Travel insurance

### Purpose-Specific Documents
**Tourist visa:**
- Tour itinerary
- Hotel bookings
- Invitation letter (agar kisi ke ghar ja rahe ho)

**Business visa:**
- Invitation letter from host company
- Business registration documents
- Employment letter

**Student visa:**
- University admission letter
- Course details
- Scholarship documents (agar applicable)

**Family/Spouse visa:**
- Marriage certificate
- Sponsor's documents

## Documents Prepare Karne Ka Process

### Step 1: Sab Documents Collect Karo

Ek checklist banao — embassy ki official website se requirements check karo. Har country ki website pe document checklist hoti hai.

**Important:** Requirements change hoti rehti hain — apply karne se pehle official embassy website zaroor check karo.

### Step 2: Physical Documents Scan Karo

Mobile scanner app use karo — Adobe Scan ya Microsoft Lens:
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI — embassy documents ke liye high quality zaroori hai
- **Color:** Color scan karo — especially passport, photos
- **Format:** PDF preferred

Scan karte waqt:
- Document flat rakho
- Proper lighting — shadows avoid karo
- Poora document frame mein aaye — edges cut na hon
- Text clearly readable ho

### Step 3: Photos Ko Sahi Format Mein Prepare Karo

Visa photos ki strict requirements hoti hain:
- White background (mostly)
- Specific dimensions (country-wise alag)
- Recent photo (6 months se purani nahi)
- No glasses, no cap

Photo JPG format mein honi chahiye. Size requirements check karo — usually 10KB-500KB.

## Country-Wise Tips

### Schengen Visa (Europe)
- Bank statement: last 3-6 months, minimum balance requirement
- Travel insurance: minimum €30,000 coverage mandatory
- Hotel bookings: cancellable bookings acceptable
- Documents: organized category-wise preferred

### USA Visa
- DS-160 form online fill hota hai
- Documents physical appointment pe le jaate hain
- Bank statements: last 3-6 months
- Ties to home country important hai

### UK Visa
- Online application, documents upload
- Bank statements: last 6 months
- Specific format requirements — embassy website check karo

### Australia Visa
- ImmiAccount pe online upload
- Documents PDF format mein
- Size limits per document

### UAE/Dubai Visa
- Travel agent ya airline ke through
- Simpler document requirements
- Bank statement: last 3 months

## Common Mistakes Jo Avoid Karni Chahiye

**Expired documents:** Passport validity check karo — most countries mein 6 months validity remaining chahiye.

**Insufficient bank balance:** Research karo ki destination country ke liye minimum balance requirement kya hai.

**Blurry scans:** Embassy documents clearly readable hone chahiye. Poor quality scan rejection ka reason ban sakta hai.

**Wrong photo specifications:** Har country ki photo requirements alag hoti hain — size, background, expression. Official requirements check karo.

**Missing documents:** Checklist carefully follow karo — ek missing document bhi rejection cause kar sakta hai.

**Old bank statements:** Fresh statements chahiye — usually last 3-6 months.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1: Kya embassy ko original documents chahiye ya copies?**
Depends on embassy aur visa type. Most tourist visas mein scanned copies/PDFs accept hoti hain online applications ke liye. Physical appointment pe originals le jaate hain verification ke liye. Embassy ki specific requirements check karo.

**Q2: Bank statement kitni purani ho sakti hai?**
Usually last 3-6 months ki statements chahiye. Schengen ke liye 3-6 months, USA ke liye 3-6 months. Exact requirement destination country ki embassy website pe check karo.

**Q3: Kya travel insurance mandatory hai?**
Schengen visa ke liye travel insurance mandatory hai (minimum €30,000 coverage). Other countries ke liye recommended hai lekin mandatory nahi. Embassy requirements check karo.

**Q4: Documents PDF mein combine karne chahiye ya alag-alag?**
Embassy ke instructions follow karo. Kuch embassies category-wise separate PDFs maangti hain (financial, personal, travel). Kuch ek combined PDF accept karti hain. Official checklist dekho.

**Q5: Visa reject ho jaaye toh kya kare?**
Rejection letter mein reason hota hai. Documents improve karo, missing information add karo, aur dobara apply karo. Kuch cases mein appeal bhi kar sakte ho. Immigration lawyer se advice lo agar zaroorat ho.
