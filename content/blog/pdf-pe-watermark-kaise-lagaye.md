## Watermark Kya Hota Hai

Watermark ek text ya image hoti hai jo PDF ke har page pe background mein dikhti hai — usually semi-transparent. Ye document ka content block nahi karta, sirf ek visual indicator hota hai.

**Common watermarks:**
- **DRAFT** — document abhi final nahi hai
- **CONFIDENTIAL** — sirf authorized log dekh sakte hain
- **SAMPLE** — ye sirf example hai, use mat karo
- **DO NOT COPY** — copying prohibited hai
- **Company logo** — branding ke liye
- **FOR REVIEW ONLY** — sirf review ke liye share kiya hai

## Watermark Lagane Ke Tips

**Opacity balance:** Bahut dark watermark content padhna mushkil kar deta hai. 20-35% opacity usually ideal hai — visible but not blocking.

**Diagonal better hai:** 45° angle pe watermark remove karna mushkil hota hai aur professional lagta hai.

**Color choice:**
- Red: urgent/important documents (CONFIDENTIAL, DRAFT)
- Grey: subtle branding
- Blue: informational (FOR REVIEW)
- Black: formal documents

**Font size:** Bada font (60-80pt) diagonal watermark ke liye better hai — chhota font tiled pattern mein better lagta hai.

**Logo watermark:** PNG format with transparent background use karo — white background wala logo PDF pe acha nahi lagta.
