# PDFOrca — Backlink Target List

> **Goal:** Build quality, relevant backlinks to improve domain authority and recover from the previous owner's toxic backlink profile.
> **Golden rule:** Quality > quantity. 10 relevant links beat 100 spammy ones. Never buy links or use PBNs — Google penalizes them.
> **Pace:** 3-5 submissions per week. Slow and steady looks natural; a sudden burst of 100 links looks like spam.
> **Track it:** Fill the Status/Date/Live-URL columns as you go.

---

## ⚠️ DO THIS FIRST (before any link building)

1. **Disavow toxic backlinks** — Search Console → Links → export external links → identify adult/Turkish spam domains → upload to Google Disavow Tool (https://search.google.com/search-console/disavow-links). Building new links while toxic ones remain is like filling a leaking bucket.
2. **Prepare your assets** (you'll reuse these everywhere):
   - **Logo:** 512x512 PNG (you have `/icon-512.png`)
   - **Short description (60 chars):** "Free, fast & private online PDF tools."
   - **Long description (150 words):** merge, split, compress, convert, sign, OCR, AI tools — privacy-first, no registration, files auto-deleted.
   - **Screenshots:** 2-3 of the homepage + a tool in action
   - **Category:** Productivity / Utilities / PDF Tools
   - **Contact email + Twitter/LinkedIn URLs**

## Anchor Text Guidance (avoid over-optimization penalty)

Vary your anchor text. Don't make every link say "compress pdf" — Google sees that as manipulation. Natural mix:

- **Branded (use most):** "PDFOrca", "pdforca.com"
- **Natural/generic:** "this free tool", "this PDF compressor", "here"
- **Partial keyword (use sparingly):** "free PDF tools", "online PDF compressor"
- **Exact keyword (rare):** "compress PDF" — only occasionally

Target ratio: ~50% branded, ~30% generic/URL, ~20% keyword-based.

## Monthly Rhythm (sustainable)

- **Week 1:** Tier 1 directories (5 submissions)
- **Week 2:** Tier 2 launch directories + set up social profiles (Tier 6)
- **Week 3:** Tier 4 community answers (3-4 genuine Quora/Reddit answers)
- **Week 4:** Tier 3/5 outreach (2-3 guest post / roundup pitches)

Repeat, rotating targets. **Never** dump 50 links in one day.

---

## What NOT to do (these hurt you)

- ❌ Buying backlinks / "1000 backlinks for $10" Fiverr gigs — instant spam signal
- ❌ PBNs (private blog networks)
- ❌ Comment spam with keyword anchors
- ❌ Same exact-match anchor text everywhere
- ❌ Getting links from adult/gambling/pirated sites (you're already recovering from this)
- ❌ Sudden link bursts — keep it gradual
