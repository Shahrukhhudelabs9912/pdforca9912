Mobile se document scan kiya — PDF ban gayi. Lekin jab text copy karne ki koshish ki, kuch nahi hua. Search kiya — nahi mila. Ye isliye hota hai kyunki scanned PDF ek image hoti hai — computer ke liye ye sirf ek photo hai, text nahi.

**OCR (Optical Character Recognition)** is problem ka solution hai. OCR technology image mein se text "padhti" hai aur use actual searchable, copyable text mein convert karti hai. PDFOrca pe ye kaam free mein hota hai.

Is guide mein bataunga ki OCR kya hota hai, scanned PDF ko editable kaise banaye, aur kab ye kaam karta hai aur kab nahi.

## OCR Kya Hota Hai — Simple Explanation

Socho tumhare paas ek handwritten letter ki photo hai. Tumhara phone us photo mein se text nahi dhundh sakta — kyunki wo sirf pixels hain, characters nahi.

OCR ek technology hai jo:
1. Image ko analyze karti hai
2. Letters aur words identify karti hai
3. Unhe actual text mein convert karti hai

Iske baad PDF mein text layer add ho jaati hai — ab tum search kar sakte ho, copy kar sakte ho, aur edit bhi kar sakte ho.

## OCR Accuracy — Kya Expect Karo

OCR accuracy depend karta hai:

**High accuracy (90-99%):**
- Clearly printed text
- Good quality scan (300 DPI+)
- Standard fonts
- Clean white background

**Medium accuracy (70-90%):**
- Slightly blurry scan
- Colored background
- Mixed fonts

**Low accuracy (below 70%):**
- Very old, faded documents
- Handwritten text
- Poor quality scan
- Damaged documents

**Handwriting:** OCR handwritten text ke saath struggle karta hai — printed text ke liye best hai.

## OCR Quality Improve Karne Ke Tips

**Better scan quality:**
- 300 DPI minimum pe scan karo
- Proper lighting — shadows avoid karo
- Document flat rakho
- Clean scanner glass

**Before OCR:**
- Agar PDF mein pages tilted hain — [PDFOrca Rotate PDF](https://pdforca.com/rotate-pdf) se seedha karo

- Important documents mein manually verify karo

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1: Kya OCR se original PDF ki quality kharab hoti hai?**
Nahi. OCR sirf ek invisible text layer add karta hai original image ke upar. Visual quality exactly same rehti hai — sirf text searchable/copyable ho jaata hai.

**Q2: Hindi text ka OCR kaam karta hai?**
Haan, PDFOrca Hindi (Devanagari) OCR support karta hai. Language selection mein Hindi choose karo. Accuracy printed Hindi text ke liye achhi hoti hai.

**Q3: Handwritten notes ka OCR ho sakta hai?**
Partially. Clear, neat handwriting ka OCR thoda kaam karta hai lekin accuracy kam hoti hai. Printed text ke liye OCR bahut better hai.

**Q4: OCR ke baad bhi text copy nahi ho raha — kya kare?**
Check karo ki OCR successfully complete hua ya nahi. Agar PDF mein text layer nahi bani, dobara try karo. Agar scan quality bahut poor hai toh better scan se try karo.

**Q5: Kya ek baar mein poori PDF ka OCR ho jaata hai?**
Haan, poori PDF ek baar mein process hoti hai — chahe 1 page ho ya 100 pages. Zyada pages mein zyada time lagta hai.
