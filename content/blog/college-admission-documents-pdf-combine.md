College admission ka time bahut stressful hota hai — deadlines, forms, documents ka dhher. Aur sabse badi problem: har college alag-alag documents maangta hai, aur aksar sab ek single PDF mein chahiye hote hain. 10th marksheet, 12th marksheet, photo, signature, caste certificate, income certificate — sab alag-alag files hain, aur inhe ek organized PDF mein combine karna padta hai.

Agar ye kaam sahi se nahi kiya toh application reject ho sakti hai ya incomplete maan li jaati hai. Is guide mein main bataunga ki college admission ke liye documents kaise prepare karo, kaise combine karo, aur size limits ke andar kaise rakho.

## College Admission Mein Kaunse Documents Chahiye Hote Hain

Har college ki requirements thodi alag hoti hain, lekin generally ye documents lagte hain:

**Academic Documents:**
- 10th marksheet aur certificate
- 12th marksheet aur certificate (ya appearing certificate)
- Transfer certificate (TC) / School leaving certificate
- Migration certificate (agar different board se ho)

**Identity Documents:**
- Aadhaar card (front + back)
- Passport size photo (usually 2-4 photos)
- Signature scan

**Category Documents (agar applicable ho):**
- Caste certificate (SC/ST/OBC)
- Income certificate
- Domicile certificate
- EWS certificate

**Other Documents:**
- Sports/NCC/NSS certificate (agar extra marks milte hain)
- Gap year affidavit (agar applicable ho)

## Documents Prepare Karne Se Pehle — Checklist

### Step 1: Sab Documents Scan Karo

Physical documents ko PDF mein convert karna padega. Best options:

**Mobile se scan karna:**
- Adobe Scan (free) — automatically crop aur enhance karta hai
- Microsoft Lens (free) — excellent quality
- CamScanner — popular option

**Scanner se:**
- 300 DPI pe scan karo — clear aur readable hoga
- Black & white documents ke liye grayscale mode use karo
- Color documents (photo, certificates) ke liye color mode

Combine karne se pehle individual files compress karo agar badi hain:
[PDFOrca Compress PDF](https://pdforca.com/compress-pdf) — quality maintain karte hue size kam karo.

## Common Mistakes Jo Avoid Karni Chahiye

**Galat order:** Colleges aksar specific order mein documents maangti hain. Admission form dhyan se padho.

**Low quality scan:** Blurry ya dark scan reject ho sakti hai. Scan karte waqt proper lighting rakho, document flat rakho.

**Wrong file format:** Kuch portals sirf PDF accept karte hain, kuch JPG bhi. Requirement check karo.

**Expired documents:** Caste certificate, income certificate — inki validity hoti hai. Purane documents mat lagao.

**Missing signature:** Kuch forms pe student ka signature chahiye — scan karke include karo.

## Alag-Alag Colleges Ke Liye Alag Sets

Agar multiple colleges mein apply kar rahe ho, har college ke liye alag PDF banao. Reason:
- Har college ki document order alag ho sakti hai
- Kuch colleges specific documents nahi maangti
- File size limits alag hoti hain

Ek master folder banao:
```
Admission_2026/
  Original_Documents/  (sab original files)
  DU_Application.pdf
  Mumbai_University_Application.pdf
  Pune_University_Application.pdf
```

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1: Kya photo ko PDF mein convert karna zaroori hai ya directly JPG upload kar sakte hain?**
Zyada tar portals sirf PDF accept karte hain. JPG ko PDF mein convert karo [PDFOrca JPG to PDF](https://pdforca.com/jpg-to-pdf) se. Kuch portals JPG bhi accept karte hain — portal ki requirements check karo.

**Q2: Merged PDF mein ek page galat aa gaya — kya dobara sab karna padega?**
Nahi. Original files se dobara merge karo sahi order mein. Original files hamesha alag save rakho — merged PDF ek final output hai.

**Q3: Aadhaar card front aur back alag pages pe hone chahiye ya ek page pe?**
Alag pages pe rakhna better hai — dono clearly readable honge. Ek page pe dono fit karne ki koshish mein text chhota ho jaata hai.

**Q4: Kya password laga sakte hain admission documents PDF pe?**
Admission portal pe upload karne wali PDF pe password mat lagao — portal read nahi kar paayega. Personal copy ke liye password laga sakte ho [PDFOrca Protect PDF](https://pdforca.com/protect-pdf) se.

**Q5: Scan ki quality kaisi honi chahiye?**
Minimum 200 DPI, ideally 300 DPI. Text clearly readable hona chahiye. Dark ya blurry scans reject ho sakti hain. Adobe Scan ya Microsoft Lens automatically quality enhance karte hain.
