## Digital Signature Vs Physical Signature

**Physical signature (purana tarika):**
1. Document print karo
2. Pen se sign karo
3. Scan ya photo lo
4. Email/upload karo
- Time: 15-20 min, printer + scanner chahiye

**Digital signature (naya tarika):**
1. PDF upload karo
2. Signature draw/upload karo

## Digital Signature Ki Legal Validity

**India mein digital signatures legally valid hain** — Information Technology Act, 2000 ke under. Lekin do types hain:

**Electronic Signature (e-signature):**
- Simple drawn/typed signature
- Zyadatar business documents ke liye valid
- PDFOrca ye type provide karta hai

**Digital Signature Certificate (DSC):**
- Government-issued cryptographic certificate
- Government tenders, MCA filings, income tax ke liye mandatory
- Ye alag hoti hai — certifying authority se leni padti hai

**Note:** Zyadatar day-to-day documents (offer letters, agreements, forms) ke liye e-signature kaafi hai. Sirf specific legal/government filings mein DSC chahiye. Important legal documents ke liye legal advice lo.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1: Kya PDFOrca ka digital signature legally valid hai?**
E-signatures India mein IT Act 2000 ke under legally valid hain zyadatar business documents ke liye. Lekin government tenders, MCA filings, aur income tax jaise specific cases mein DSC (Digital Signature Certificate) chahiye, jo alag hoti hai. Important legal documents ke liye legal advice lo.

**Q2: Signature ki photo upload karu ya draw karu — kaunsa better hai?**
Dono theek hain. Draw karna quick hai. Photo upload consistent signature deta hai (same har document pe). Business documents ke liye uploaded signature professional lagta hai.

**Q3: Kya signed PDF edit ho sakti hai baad mein?**
Signature add karne ke baad PDF normal PDF hi rehti hai. Koi bhi edit kar sakta hai theoretically. High-security documents ke liye password lagao ya certified digital signature (DSC) use karo.

**Q4: Kya main dusre ki bheji PDF pe sign karke wapas bhej sakta hu?**
Haan, bilkul. Koi bhi PDF upload karo, sign karo, download karo, wapas bhejo. Yahi iska main use case hai — offer letters, agreements.

**Q5: Multiple log ek document pe sign kar sakte hain?**
Haan. Pehla person sign karke bheje, doosra usi signed PDF ko dobara upload karke apna signature add kare. Har person same tool use kar sakta hai.
