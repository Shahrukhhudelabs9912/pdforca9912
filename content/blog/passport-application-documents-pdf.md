Passport banwana hai — pehli baar ya renewal — aur documents ka dhher hai. Aadhaar, birth certificate, address proof, photo, signature — sab alag-alag files hain. Passport Seva portal pe upload karne ke liye sab ek organized PDF mein chahiye, specific size limits ke saath. Agar documents sahi format mein nahi hue toh application reject ho sakti hai ya delay ho sakti hai.

Is guide mein bataunga ki passport application ke liye documents kaise prepare karo, PDF mein kaise combine karo, aur size limits ke andar kaise rakho — taaki pehli baar mein hi application accept ho jaaye.

## Passport Application Ke Liye Kaunse Documents Chahiye

### Fresh Passport (Pehli Baar)

**Identity Proof (koi ek):**
- Aadhaar card
- Voter ID
- PAN card

**Address Proof (koi ek):**
- Aadhaar card (agar address current hai)
- Electricity/water/gas bill (last 3 months)
- Bank passbook (first page)
- Rent agreement (notarized)

**Date of Birth Proof (koi ek):**
- Birth certificate
- 10th marksheet (date of birth wali)
- School leaving certificate

**Other:**
- Passport size photos (2 copies — physical)
- Signature on white paper (scan ke liye)

### Passport Renewal

- Old passport (original + photocopy)
- Aadhaar card
- Address proof (agar address change hua ho)

### Tatkal Passport

Upar wale sab + additional documents:
- Verification certificate from employer/gazetted officer
- Specific documents as per category

## Passport Seva Portal Pe Upload Requirements

Passport Seva portal pe documents upload karne ki requirements:

| Document | Format | Size Limit |
|----------|--------|------------|
| Photo | JPG | 10KB – 500KB |
| Signature | JPG | 10KB – 500KB |
| Supporting documents | PDF/JPG | 1MB per document |

*Note: Requirements change hoti hain — apply karte waqt official portal check karo: passportindia.gov.in*

## Documents Prepare Karne Ka Process

### Step 1: Physical Documents Scan Karo

Mobile scanner app use karo — Adobe Scan ya Microsoft Lens:
- **Resolution:** 200-300 DPI
- **Color:** Grayscale (text documents ke liye), Color (photo ID ke liye)
- **Format:** PDF ya JPG

Scan karte waqt:
- Document flat rakho — table pe
- Proper lighting — shadows avoid karo
- Poora document frame mein aaye — edges cut na hon
- Text clearly readable ho

Passport photo requirements:
- White background

## Common Mistakes Jo Avoid Karni Chahiye

**Blurry scan:** Passport office documents clearly readable maangta hai. Blurry ya dark scan rejection ka reason ban sakta hai.

**Expired documents:** Address proof 3 months se purana nahi hona chahiye. Birth certificate ki koi expiry nahi hoti.

**Wrong photo:** White background mandatory hai. Colored background, sunglasses, ya cap wali photo reject hogi.

**File too large:** 1MB limit strict hai. Compress karo — [PDFOrca Compress PDF](https://pdforca.com/compress-pdf).

**Name mismatch:** Sab documents mein naam exactly same hona chahiye — spelling, middle name sab. Agar alag hai toh affidavit lagta hai.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1: Kya Aadhaar card hi kaafi hai passport ke liye?**
Aadhaar identity aur address proof dono ke liye use ho sakta hai — agar address current hai. Birth date proof ke liye alag document chahiye (birth certificate ya 10th marksheet).

**Q2: Passport photo ka size exactly kitna hona chahiye?**
Physical photo: 35mm x 45mm, white background. Digital upload ke liye: JPG format, 10KB-500KB size. Face clearly visible, recent photo.

**Q3: Documents PDF mein combine karne chahiye ya alag-alag upload karne chahiye?**
Passport Seva portal pe har document type alag upload hota hai — Aadhaar alag, birth certificate alag. Sirf ek document ke front+back ko combine karo. Portal ki instructions follow karo.

**Q4: Scan ki quality kaisi honi chahiye?**
Minimum 200 DPI, ideally 300 DPI. Text clearly readable hona chahiye. Passport office reject kar sakta hai agar document properly visible nahi hai.

**Q5: Kya mobile se documents upload kar sakte hain?**
Haan, Passport Seva portal mobile pe bhi kaam karta hai. Documents prepare karne ke liye [PDFOrca](https://pdforca.com) mobile browser mein use karo — compress, merge, convert sab mobile pe hota hai.
